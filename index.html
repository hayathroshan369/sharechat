How to create an account in Sharechat?

Step 1 - First of all open your Sharechat App.As soon as you open the sharechat application,you will be asked to select the language.
Step 2 - You can select the language in which you keep your interest,now a new page opens in front of you.In that page you are asked for some personal details which have to be filled.
Step 3 - In personal details they are asked for your name,gender,mobile number etc...And after that you see an adult post option.
Step 4 - After doing this,you get to see a submit button below.Click on that submit option.
Step 5 - As soon as you click on the option of submit,then the OTP is sent by th sharechat app on the mobile number given by you,enter that OTP.
Step 6 - After the mobile number is verified,your account is completely created and after that you can use the Sharechat App.
